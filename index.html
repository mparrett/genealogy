About This Project

This site documents four family lines converging in the American Southwest—tracing ancestors from Tennessee, Germany, and Ireland through generations of migration and settlement.

Family tree profiles and genetic genealogy data are also maintained on Ancestry, WikiTree, FamilySearch, and 23andMe.
