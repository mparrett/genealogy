Gleeson / Fitzgerald Line

Ireland → Wisconsin → Arizona

The Gleeson and Fitzgerald families emigrated from County Limerick, Ireland during the Famine era. They settled in Buffalo County, Wisconsin, where Irish Catholic farming communities flourished. Their descendants eventually moved west to Arizona.
