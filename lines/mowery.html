Mowery Line

Tennessee → Texas → Arizona

The Mowery line traces from early 19th-century Tennessee through the Texas frontier to 20th-century Arizona. Beginning with Lewis R. Mowery in Bradley County, Tennessee, the family moved west with the great migrations, eventually settling in Williams, Arizona along Route 66.
