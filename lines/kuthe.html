Kuthe Line

Germany → Missouri → Oregon → Arizona

The Kuthe line begins with Frederick Kuthe's emigration from Vöhl, Hesse, Germany to Missouri in the mid-19th century. His descendants moved west through Oregon's Willamette Valley before eventually settling in Arizona.
