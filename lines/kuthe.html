Kuthe Line

Germany → Missouri → Oregon → Arizona

The Kuthe line begins in Saxony, Germany, emigrating to Missouri in the mid-19th century. Frederick Kuthe established the family in America, and subsequent generations moved westward through Oregon before settling in Arizona.
