Higgins / Knight Line

Dublin → Newark → San Francisco

The Higgins line traces from Rathmines, Dublin to industrial Newark, New Jersey, and eventually to California. Laurence Higgins worked as a porter in Newark, while his son James became a civil engineer on the Western Pacific Railroad.
