## Matt's Direct Lineage (Mowrey Line)

```
Johann Peter Maurer (1709–1781) + Anna Sophia Germann — immigrated 1751 on ship Edinburgh
    └── Peter W. Mowrey (1760–1840) + Gibson(?) — Revolutionary War veteran, Battle of Guilford Court House
        └── Lewis R. Mowery (c.1801–1850) + Elizabeth Lisbee
            └── Moses Mansfield Mowery (1822–1904) + Margaret Witt
                └── Samuel S. Mowrey (1867–1904) + Effie Mae Coker (1878–1958)
                    └── Raymond Robert Mowrey (1899–1973) + Anne Barbara Gleeson (1903–1986)
                        └── Patricia Anne Mowrey (1929–2009) ← grandmother
                            └── Michele Birch/Parrett ← mother
                                └── Matt
```

**Notes:**
- Surname evolved: Maurer → Mowrey/Mowery as family moved south
- Johann Peter: German immigrant from Baumholder, Rheinland; probate Lancaster, PA 1781
- Peter W.: Pension S1568, DAR Ancestor #A131224; will in Knox Estate Book 7, p.17
- Raymond + Anne Barbara Gleeson marriage connects Mowrey and Gleeson lines

## Image Styles
- **Bio illustrations**: Watercolor style, warm sepia tones, narrative moments
- **Location panels**: Hand-watercolored engraving style (linework + subtle color washes)
  - Reference: https://mirka-h.blogspot.com/2015/02/imprinting-american-west-engravings.html
  - Techniques: parallel lines, cross-hatching, stippling; watercolor applied to complement not overwhelm linework
- **Future**: May become a print book — keep high-res originals, design for dual web/print use
